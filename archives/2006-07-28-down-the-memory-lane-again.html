Maybe because I have nothing better to do, but during the past week , it seems like I had quite a few brushes with the memory lane.

I found my old 'collections' folder in at the bottom of bookshelf. It has like a lot of old new-paper cuttings. I used to cut this article about geograhpy every week. Ironically, I never took geography for O levels..

I also found my autograph of Aishwarya Rai, that was taken at Orchard road..:)

I also dug up the old storage box from the store. I needed to clean it and put some NUS stuff in it. But I ended up looking at the contents and remembering old times for the whole day...

I found old school magazines. School photos..


I found my old music book..

" Chan mali chan, chan mali chan,
Chan mali chan, ketipung payung."

And the icing on the cake was when I visited Far Horizon Gardens after so many years!!..

I have so many memories of that place. All the events.. all the people...

I love to keep old things. They bring back memories. They let you touch your own past and bring you back to your childhood.

I miss those childhood days. Not a care in the world!!
