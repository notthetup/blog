A chat with a good friend, a post in a blog, a speech by a fellow Toastmaster, and a long bus ride home.

They all seemed to say the same thing to me. Like Jobs said, "If today were the last day of my life, would I want to do what I am about to do today?"

The answer has been "NO" for so many days! I should really change something.

I have always believed in living life without any regrets, and yet now I have some.

With this perspective, what seems really stupid is the really long ToDo list I have, which I have been trying to reduce, to no avail. I always say, "One day I will do this."
One day.... Hopefully the day arrives, before everything is lost!
